Who We Are

Music Is For Everyone

Ghostbuskers is a community music project based in Colwyn Bay, run by TAPE Community Music and Film. We perform at public events — streets, parks, festivals — and our performances have a twist: they don't end when the music stops. They end when the audience becomes part of the band.

Whether you've played music all your life or you've never picked up an instrument, you are welcome here. Our "Gooboos" come from every background, every age, every ability. There are no auditions. There is no wrong way to join in.

We meet every Wednesday, 6–7pm at TAPE in Old Colwyn — and online via Zoom for those who can't make it in person. Sessions are pay as you feel (suggested £2).

The Ghostbuskers Way

How A Gig Works

01

We Show Up

We arrive at a public place — a street, a park, a festival — with our instruments and our songbooks.

02

You Watch

A crowd gathers. People stop, listen, enjoy the music. A normal beginning.

03

You Join In

We hand out songbooks and instruments. One by one, the audience becomes the band.

04

No More Audience

By the end, everyone is playing. There are no spectators — only musicians.
